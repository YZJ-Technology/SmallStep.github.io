Details of Data Handling

Data Collection and Usage

When utilizing the Service, Usage Data is gathered automatically. This may involve details like the Internet Protocol address (IP address) of your device, the type of browser you use, its version, which pages of our Service you visit, your visiting time and date, the duration of your visit on those pages, unique device identifiers, and additional diagnostic information.

Should you use a mobile device to access the Service, we automatically gather specific data. This includes but is not limited to the kind of mobile device you employ, its unique ID, the IP address of your mobile device, your mobile operating system, the mobile internet browser you utilize, unique device identifiers, and more diagnostic data.

Furthermore, each time you visit our Service or access it using a mobile device, your browser may transmit certain information to us. The following is an indicative, but not exhaustive, list of Usage Data components:

- Model of hardware;

- Operating system details and version;

- Screen dimensions and pixel density;

- Language and country setting;

- Unique device identifiers (such as IDFA);

- Time zone setting;

- Status of device sound settings (pertinent to reminder functionalities).

For enhanced user experience, we have incorporated tools for data analytics within the App. These tools do not gather health or private data of users. They are solely utilized for pinpointing CRASH issues and conducting AB tests for new features and designs. The information collected includes:

- Details about the device, such as:

- Screen Size

- Manufacturer

- Model

- System information of the device:

- OS Name

- OS Version

- OS Build

- Time Zone

- System Language

- Carrier's country and name

- Location details:

- Locale

- Country and regional area

- Information regarding the application:

- App Name

- Build of the App

- Version number of the App

- SDK Name

- SDK Version.
